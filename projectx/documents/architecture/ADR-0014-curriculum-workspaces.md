# ADR-0014 — Curriculum Workspaces

## Status
Accepted

## Context

Project X is evolving from a curriculum database into a Teaching Operating System. Teachers need operational workspaces, not disconnected pages.

## Decision

The Curriculum Intelligence Centre will support context-aware workspaces for units and lessons. Lessons open as Digital Lesson Twin workspaces with common tabs, context, readiness, resources, assessment, evidence, reflection, AI and history.

## Consequences

- The Lesson Workspace becomes the central teaching cockpit.
- Future modules should deep-link into the Lesson Workspace instead of duplicating lesson data.
- The workspace frame, ribbon, tabs and context panel become reusable UI patterns.
