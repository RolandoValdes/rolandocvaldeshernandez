Curriculum and Assessment

Curriculum design

My curriculum work focuses on clear sequencing, retrieval, explicit modelling, adaptive teaching, assessment for learning and practical application. I use interactive resources and dashboards to make learning visible and to support progress tracking.

Curriculum areas

  • Programming and algorithms
  • Computer systems and networks
  • Data representation
  • Cybersecurity and ethics
  • Databases and SQL
  • Robotics and physical computing

Assessment practice

  • Question-level analysis
  • Exam-style modelling
  • Trace tables and worked examples
  • Feedback and targeted intervention
  • Interactive assessment dashboards
