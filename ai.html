AI, Innovation and Digital Learning

AI-supported teaching practice

I use AI as a professional planning and differentiation aid, while maintaining teacher judgement, safeguarding awareness and curriculum integrity.

Tools and workflows

  • ChatGPT for resource drafting, adaptation and modelling
  • NotebookLM for study guides and knowledge organisation
  • Eduaide-style planning, rubrics and questioning support
  • HTML/JSON dashboards for interactive evidence and assessment
  • Python, Tkinter and SQLite for internal teaching tools

Impact

AI and automation allow faster resource iteration, more tailored support, clearer evidence organisation and more accessible learning materials for students and colleagues.
